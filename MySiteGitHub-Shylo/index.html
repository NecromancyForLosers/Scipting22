Shylo Olsen

Shylo Olsen is a first year at Lethbridge college. She grew up in Raymond and wasn’t as financially stable as everyone else in her class so she didn’t have as much access to amenities as her peers but despite this roadblock, she continued to pursue her passion for art. Her attention at the moment is focused on her comic, she has been working on it since she was 14 years old. Her many hobbies include Digital art, video editing, playing games, reading books, writing stories, and watching youtube. When Shylo was younger she struggled with her queer identity and because of that, she grew up wanting to change the media’s portrayal or lack of portrayal of queer and POC characters. Philosophy and nonfiction are Shylo’s favorite book genres because they force her to look at life through a different lens, which she finds helps her write more diverse and interesting characters in her work. She views nature in adoration and seeks to better the environment by being as eco-friendly as possible. She is often ranting and raving about big corporations and their contribution to pollution in our skies and oceans. She often considers herself lucky to have grown up in such a creatively motivated time period, where tv shows could portray in-depth stories without being penalized for doing so. Shylo one day hopes to inspire people with her work and stories, opening people's eyes to experiences that they wouldn't necessarily face or understand. In the end, Shylo just wants to make the world a little better.
