Geography

Lethbridge is well known for its rolling coulees and proximity to the mountains. There are many animals that are native to Lethbridge such as Painted Turtles, Moose, bighorn sheep, wolves, mountain goats, lynxes, and coyotes. A lot of the forestry is located in the coulees, where you can go bridge jumping or go on a walk through the trees. Due to the town's proximity to the mountains Lethbridge experiences warm summers and colder winters. Often southern Alberta experiences Chinooks, which are a warm wind, and Lethbridge is no exception.
