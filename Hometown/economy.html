Economy

Students make up a lot of Lethbridge’s population as Lethbridge College is famously known for its many programs that go above and beyond many other schools you’ll find. It’s mostly well known for its exceptional Criminal Justice program and Nursing programs. A large portion of Lethbridge business is locally owned.
